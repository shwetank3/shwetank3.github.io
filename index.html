About Me

Tseng-Hung Chen is a graduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University, working with Prof. Min Sun.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. My research projects focus on natural language generation and visual semantic understanding. In particular, I was interested in end-to-end sequence learning with Convolutional / Recurrent Neural Networks architectures. Recently, I am interested in Generative Adversarial Networks.

See my CV for more details.
